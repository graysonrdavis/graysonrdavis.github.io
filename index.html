About

I am a first-year PhD student in the Courant Institute of Mathematical Sciences at New York University (NYU). I am originally from Coquitlam, BC, Canada, and received my BSc (Hons.) in Mathematics from Simon Fraser University (SFU) in 2024. In my free time, I like to watch anime, go for walks/hikes, and check out the newest hit musicals on Broadway.

Research Interests

I am currently interested in the analysis of partial differential equations and computer-assisted proofs. My undergraduate research background is in the areas of kinetic equations and numerical spectral geometry.
